Labmeeting

Information about our lab meeting
Author

MG

Published

March 24, 2025

We meet every week in the corner office, alternating between Labmeetings and “Lunch & Science”. You can see the schedule on the calendar.

In labmeetings, we discuss organisatory issues and everyone can bring up potential topics or problems to discuss. Additionally, we do presentations of our work, discuss papers,…

For “Lunch & Science”, everyone gathers in the corner office with something to eat and we watch a talk related to our research.
